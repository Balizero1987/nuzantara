## Critical Signals

### Healthcare (UU 36/2009)
- `healthcare_type`: public, private, traditional
- `license_required`: true/false
- `practitioner_requirements`: WNI only, WNA allowed
- `insurance_coverage`: BPJS, private

### BPJS (UU 24/2011)
- `mandatory_for`: WNI, WNA with KITAS, employers
- `contribution_rate`: percentage of salary
- `coverage_type`: Kesehatan, Ketenagakerjaan
- `benefits`: what's covered
- `penalties`: for non-compliance

### Education (UU 20/2003, PP 47/2008)
- `education_level`: SD, SMP, SMA, universitas
- `mandatory_years`: 9 years, 12 years
- `foreign_schools`: requirements for WNA children
- `teacher_requirements`: WNI only, WNA with permit

## WNI/WNA Considerations

- BPJS mandatory for WNA with work permit
- Foreign children can attend international schools
- Healthcare access equal for WNI and WNA with valid visa
