How it works

Click or drag the switch to toggle between day and night modes. The circular knob slides smoothly from left to right with a satisfying animation.

Day Mode (OFF): Knob on the left with red-to-orange gradient

Night Mode (ON): Knob slides to the right with purple-to-cyan gradient and glow effect

Pros: Standard, familiar toggle pattern. Smooth animation. Compact size (50px).

Cons: Less explicit than text labels (on/off doesn't clearly indicate day/night without context). Some users may not immediately understand the mapping.
